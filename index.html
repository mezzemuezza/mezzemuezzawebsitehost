About

We believe food is more than just a meal, it’s a shared experience. We bring you a menu that celebrates fresh ingredients, fusion recipes, and generous hospitality.

Mezze Muezza is inspired by two things we love. Mezze – flavour packed dishes made for sharing in true Lebanese dining. Muezza - the name of a much-loved cat in middle eastern tales symbolising comfort, kindness, and care.

Every dish we prepare is crafted with love. Whether you’re in need of a good feed or a catered event, come find our food truck and let us treat you like family.
